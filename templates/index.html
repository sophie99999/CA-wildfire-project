California Wildfire 101

The Camp Fire was the deadliest and most destructive wildfire incidents in California history and the most expensive natual disaster in the world in 2018 in terms of insured losses. Named after Camp Creek Road, its place of origin, the fire started on November 8, 2018, in Northen Califonia and almost destroyed the whole town called Paradise.

The Camp Fire is just one of many wildfire incidents in California. The causes of wildfire varies,including dry weather, power lines and electrical equipment, arson and cigarettes. Our team is going to investigate and visualize the correlation between frequency of wildfire and other factors in California. We are going to focus on the weather factor given the timeframe.
